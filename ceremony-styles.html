Ceremony Options

Ceremony Styles

Every couple is different. Your ceremony should reflect the tone, values, family, faith, and meaning behind the commitment being made.

A Ceremony That Fits the Moment

Wedding Solutions offers chaplain-led ceremonies that can be traditional, civil, faith-friendly, Christian-informed, family-centered, patriotic, simple, formal, or fully customized. The goal is to create a ceremony that feels sincere, orderly, and worthy of the vows being exchanged.

Traditional Wedding Ceremony

A dignified ceremony built around commitment, vows, family, and the public meaning of marriage. This style works well for couples who want a timeless ceremony with structure, warmth, and seriousness.

Civil Wedding Ceremony

A respectful, nonreligious ceremony for couples who want something elegant, professional, and legally sound without religious language. Simple does not have to mean empty.

Faith-Friendly Ceremony

A ceremony that may include prayer, blessing, Scripture, or references to God while remaining welcoming and accessible for guests from different backgrounds.

Christian-Informed Ceremony

A ceremony rooted more directly in Christian language, covenant, prayer, biblical principles, and the sacred nature of marriage.

Veteran & First Responder Ceremony

A ceremony that honors service, duty, sacrifice, courage, and commitment. This option can be especially meaningful for military, law enforcement, fire, EMS, and first responder families.

Blended Family Ceremony

A ceremony that may include children, family vows, unity elements, or a special acknowledgment of the family being joined. This style is thoughtful for second marriages and families beginning a new chapter together.

Elopement Ceremony

A simple, meaningful, legally sound ceremony for couples who want something smaller, quieter, and less complicated than a full formal wedding.

Custom Ceremony

A personalized ceremony built around the couple’s story, values, vows, family traditions, readings, faith elements, or special moments they want included.
