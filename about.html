About Wedding Solutions

Chaplain-Led Ceremonies with Dignity and Purpose

Wedding Solutions was created to provide couples in Rochester, Monroe County, and Western New York with meaningful wedding ceremonies rooted in respect, commitment, family, faith sensitivity, and legal professionalism.

A Steady Voice for an Important Moment

Marriage Deserves More Than a Generic Script

A wedding ceremony is more than a formality. It is a public declaration, a family moment, a legal act, and for many couples, a sacred commitment. It deserves to be handled with care, clarity, and steady leadership.

As an ordained chaplain, public speaker, veteran, and community leader, I bring a calm and professional presence to the ceremony. My goal is to help each couple create a ceremony that reflects their values while honoring the seriousness of the vows being made.

Whether your ceremony is civil, traditional, faith-friendly, Christian-informed, family-centered, simple, formal, or fully customized, Wedding Solutions is built to serve couples who want the moment handled properly.

What Couples Can Expect

  • Professional ceremony leadership
  • Respect for marriage, family, and tradition
  • Faith-sensitive and civil ceremony options
  • Clear communication before the wedding day
  • Support with vows, ceremony flow, and license awareness
  • A calm presence when the room needs one

Our Approach

Built on Service, Trust, and Commitment

Every ceremony is approached with practical preparation, respect for the couple, and a clear understanding that the wedding day is not about performance. It is about the promises being made.

Dignity

The ceremony should reflect the importance of marriage and the seriousness of the vows being exchanged.

Clarity

Couples should understand the ceremony process, the package selected, and the marriage license requirements before the wedding day.

Faith Sensitivity

Ceremonies can include prayer, Scripture, blessing, or faith language when desired, while also offering respectful civil options.

Professionalism

From planning conversations to ceremony delivery, the goal is to bring calm, confidence, and order to an important day.

Experience That Matters

That background matters when standing before families and guiding one of the most meaningful parts of a wedding day.
