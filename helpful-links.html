Resources for Married Life

Helpful Links for Couples

Marriage is more than a ceremony. These local and regional resources may help couples plan responsibly for licensing, homeownership, financial stability, parenting, and family life.

A Practical Starting Point

Next Steps Wedding Solutions provides these links as a convenience for couples in Rochester, Monroe County, Western New York, and the Finger Lakes. Outside organizations are not controlled by Next Steps Wedding Solutions, and program details may change.

Couples should contact each organization directly to confirm eligibility, current services, costs, requirements, appointments, and availability.

Important Ceremony and Booking Reminder

Couples are responsible for obtaining a valid New York State marriage license before the ceremony. The license must be valid and available for the ceremony to be legally performed.

Submission of an inquiry does not reserve a ceremony date. Dates are reserved only after availability is confirmed, the agreement is completed, and the required retainer is received.
